Yogacharya Vinayak
 
Yogacharya Vinayak is an able exponent of the Yogic way of life and has had a salutary influence on many people from all over the world who come to him for counseling or learning. He has traveled widely over the length and breadth of India as well as various European nations. His rich experience is a synthesis of various wonderful techniques of some great masters. His extreme simplicity and remarkable insight make him a unique person.

Yogacharya Vinayak was associated with Vivekananda Kendra, Kanyakumari, as a life worker (Jeevan Vrati) for various types of social work for 8 years. At its various branches, he taught ancient Yogic wisdom and assimilated the techniques of various schools of Yoga from traditional masters and also from Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana, Deemed University. Then, he pursued M.sc in Yoga Therapy from S-VYASA Deemed University Bangalore He has an experience of conducting yoga professionally for more than 29 years.

Yogacharya Vinayak worked at many Luxury and heritage hotels:
  • Rambagh Palace, Taj group of hotels for 3 years
  • ITC Rajputna Palace(formerly Sheraton Rajaputna Palace hotel) Jaipur, 5-star luxury hotel for 1 year as Yogacharya
  • As Director of Yoga and Health at the National Award Winner, Khimsar Fort Heritage Hotel of Welcome Group in Rajasthan

Sri Yogacharya Vinayak was a “Visiting Faculty” at OTS (Officer Training School), Rajasthan State Institute of Public Administration. He was a Yoga consultant at Maharani Gayatri Devi School Yogacharya Vinayak has published research papers in national & international conferences.
His recent publications include:
  • “Yoga in Pre-school and Primary School curriculum” which has been published in the proceedings of 1st international Conference on ‘Yoga in Education’ [ICYE-2012], November 27-28, 2012, Tumkur University, Tumkur.
  • In 2010, he had published another paper “Effect of control diet on Asthama” in the proceedings of National conference on Sanskrit Text of Ayurveda and Yoga (AYUR YOG) 2010, University of Hyderabad.

IIT Kanpur has felicitated him 6 times for conducting workshop on various topics related to Yoga. He has conducted classes in France, Germany and Switzerland.
Other places where he conducted Yoga workshops/classes:
  • Banks, Universities, Technical colleges, Chartered Accountant’s association, Airport Authority of India etc.,
  • National companies like JK White cement and Bajaj Auto
  • Software companies like Vitesse Semiconductor Pvt. Ltd and Perceptive Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
  • Indian Institute of Health Management and Research Center Jaipur

He was appreciated for conducting Yoga classes thoughout the day for more than 100 participants at "20th international conference on Artificial Intelligence (ICAI)" at IIIT-H in 2007

He has been working as Yogacharya in IIIT-H for the past 10 years. He is involved in research and development of a comprehensive database for an interactive online guidance system of yoga therapy and its application to various ailments.

He has established Yuktahar Kendra (click here)

IIIT-H management had sent him to Jaipur to complete a condensed course on Naturopathy. And then onwards IIIT-H had given him the responsibility to run the Naturopathy center where Steam, Sauna, Massage, Mud therapy, Wax therapy etc., He contributed to Sanskar School by developing more than 50 yogic games for Panchakosha. These games help in overall development on physical, vital, mental, intellectual and spiritual unfoldment. He used to conduct the workshops for personality development, Concentration, memory development, leadership skills etc.

Yogacharya has designed a chair ‘Asan’ which helps a person to maintain the right posture of sitting for long hours without back pain.
Yogacharya was selected to train Yoga/PT teachers of govt. schools, by PSS Central Institute of Vocational Education (NCERT, Under MHRD, govt. of India).
