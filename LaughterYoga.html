Laughter Yoga:
Unlimited Happiness through Laughter Yoga:
Today life is very stressful and stress related diseases are on the rise like Depression, Anxiety, High blood pressure, Nervous breakdowns, Heart diseases, Ulcers, etc.

As a matter of fact, the incidence of cancer has been attributed to the stress of modern life. It has been proved by researchers all over the world that laughter is a much easier form of stress busting.

It has long been recognized that stress weakens the immune system, leaving you more vulnerable to illness. You have been probably noticed yourself that you simply feel better after a good belly laugh.

This class includes playful interactive group laughter exercises with stretching. This session teaches you how to laugh whole-heartily and provides you a safe space for you to tap in your inner child by letting go of judgment, negativity, hostility, fear, and stress and replacing these feelings with love, peace, acceptance and joy.

Process of laughter therapy: Laughter Therapy deals with un-conditional laughter, combining with yogic breathing. Anyone can laugh without relying on humor, jokes or comedy. Laughter is initially simulated as a physical exercise while maintaining eye contact with others in the group and promoting childlike playfulness. In most cases, this process leads to real and contagious laughter.

Science has proven that the body cannot differentiate between simulated and real laughter.
  • As the person laughs, carbon dioxide is driven out of the body to be replaced by oxygen-rich air, providing physical and mental freshness
  • It can produce anti-inflammatory agents, which can avoid back pain or arthritis
  • It encourages muscle to relax, and exercises muscles all over the body, from the scalp to the legs.
  • It reduces levels of the stress hormones
  • It aids immune system responses
  • It exercises facial muscles to prevent sagging
  • It boosts the production of ‘feel-good’ endorphin hormones.
