Back Pain:


Reason for backpain:
  1. Spasm
  2. Convex Lumbar Region
  3. Improper sitting posture
    • Improper arm rest
    • Lack of support to lumbar region
    • Improper distance between back rest and knee
  4. Weak muscles
  5. Improper lifting/li>
  6. Improper bed
  7. Hamstring stiffness
  8. Stress
How is it handled?:
There are special yogic techniques exclusively for backpain which can handle many of above reasons. These techniques not help in relaxing the back muscles but also in strengthening them.
We also detail you regarding right posture, diet, dealing with stress situations, etc.
A test is conducted to analysis the intensity of your backpain and to gauge the strength/weakness in your back muscles.
The above mentioned test is conducted twice, once at the beginning and the other at the end to make you realise overall improvement of your back.
