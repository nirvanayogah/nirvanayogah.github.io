General class on Yoga and Pranayama (Breathing Techniques):
A short package that teaches how to keep the body and mind healthy by exercising the entire body and improving the quality of life and well-being. It develops concentration, confidence, efficiency and more.
Yogic Purification Techniques:
These purify the internal body from the Nose and mouth to the anus and genital organs. These techniques also enhance concentration and confidence; and help get rid of many diseases.
Yogic Meditation Techniques:
Techniques to help concentrate, purify thoughts, attain peace of mind and allow self analysis. These develop overall personality and health, achieve blissful awareness and for ultimate freedom.
Yogic Relaxation Techniques:
These are very useful for busy professionals who are involved in heavy mental/physical work and also for patients of insomnia, heart disease, exhaustion and such.
Spiritual Retreat:
This program includes techniques for spiritual development, such as advance Yogasans, Pranayama, Mudras, Bandhas, Trataka and purification methods. It also incorporates knowledge sharing, Vedic chanting, bhajan (devotional session), study circle, meditation techniques and question-answer session etc.
Yogic Stress Management:
A program for stress and tension reduction. Stress is the required stimulation to face a situation. When the stress increases more than a certain limit it creates problems. To reduce the stress, we need to calm down the mind with the help of Yogasans and other relaxation techniques. There are three types of relaxation techniques; Instant relaxation technique (3 minutes), Quick relaxation technique (5 minutes) and Deep relaxation technique (15 minutes). 50 minutes of relaxation can give the soothing effect of 4 hours of deep sleep. By controlling one’s anxiety, one can bring joy and peace in life.
Yogic Breathing Techniques:
The program includes various breathing techniques to help develop concentration, stability and also purify & calm down the mind. These techniques help to reduce the blood pressure, asthma, insomnia and eliminate psychosomatic disorder. They also bring cheer and joy.
Yogic Bala-Sanskar Varga:
This program is designed to educate the children and facilitate their overall personality development with the help of games, songs, Yogasans etc. It also helps improve concentration, creativity, confidence, efficiency and more.
Personality Development Camp:
This facilitates overall personality development at physical, mental, emotional, intellectual and spiritual levels with the help of group discussions, presentations, street plays, songs, games, trekking, Surya-namaskar (Sun-Salutation), knowledge sharing etc.
Workshops:
(For Professionals, Management Cadre, Office Staff, Workers, Students etc.) These include packages on stress management, yogic games for concentration and defocusing of mind, creativity and efficiency development. Special games to improve inter-personal relationship, to overcome inferiority complex, to discover our latent talents and to understand our own mistakes and lacunas, to better understand ourselves, to awaken the inner self for excellence in work, to trigger employee productivity and motivation, de-stress work environment & people – in short, ideas for managing the life in a better way. It includes knowledge sharing and question-answer sessions
Yoga for Senior Citizen:
These include easy techniques which can be performed in the office, while traveling and even on the bed. Through this one can get rid of toxins and tone-up muscles. This program is very good for arthritis, combating worries & feeling of insecurity, giving meaning & purpose to life, fighting depression and loneliness.
