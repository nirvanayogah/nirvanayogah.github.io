Nirvana

The word Nirvana comes from the root meaning 'to blow out' and refers to the extinguishing of the fires of greed, hatred and delusion. When these emotional and psychological defilements are destroyed by wisdom, the mind becomes free, radiant and joyful and at death one is no longer subject to rebirth word nirvana to means "liberation" or "unbinding". Nirvana is liberation, but liberation from what? The standard answer is "samsara," which usually is defined as the cycle of birth, death, and rebirth. Nirvana is also said to be liberation from dukkha,or the stress/pain/dissatisfaction of life. It is more like a state of existence.

“Nirvana is beyond space, time, and definition.”

“Nirvana is the ultimate happiness" (nibbanam paramam sukham)
