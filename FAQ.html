FAQ / Precaution:


  1. What yoga-asanas should I perform? In what sequence should I perform them?
    • a. The asanas and their sequence should be selected depending on the age, gender, condition of the health and purpose. For that one should take the guidance of the proper teacher.

  2. What is the significance of doing complementary asanas?
    • a. In order to prevent lopsided development, one must perform complement of every asana.
    • b. The asanas should be performed without forcing or jerking the body. One should reach the final state slowly.

  3. For how long should the final position of asanas be maintained?
    • a. Try to maintain the final for as long as possible depending on the cause.
    • b. Don’t over-strain yourself.
    • c. In-order to reap maximum benefit from the asanas, perform Shavasana at the end.
    • d. Don’t compete with others because ability of everyone person differs.

  4. How much time should one give between having food and performing Yoga-asanas?
    • a. The asanas must be always performed ideally with empty stomach or at least three and half hours after having food. One can have food ideally after two and half hours or at least half an hour after yoga session.
    • b. Don’t over-strain yourself.
    • c. In order to train the mind, do yoga-asanas with awareness.

  5. What precautions should be taken in case of accidents, operations or other diseases?
    • a. Please consult your family doctor and also a good yoga teacher before performing any asana. Some asanas can be performed only when you are normal.
    • b. While performing asanas please their limitations in mind.
    • c. Please perform Asanas in a clean and well ventilated room. Don’t perform in strong breeze.
    • d. If you are doing exercise and yoga-asanas together then perform exercise first followed by Shavasana for 20 minutes and then yoga-asanas.
    • e. Please wear comfortable (lose) clothes while performing asanas.
    • f. Ladies shouldn’t perform any heavy exercises or asanas during periods. Pregnant ladies should perform asanas only after consulting.
