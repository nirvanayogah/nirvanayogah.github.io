Improvement of sixth sense:
We experience the world with the help of our five sense organs those are namely Eyes, ears, nose, Skin, and tongue. Mind analyses these experiences and then correlates it with previous experiences. During to this process, some understanding is developed which determines how we perceive the experience either as good or bad.

In either of the cases our mind gets involved in planning about how to retain or achieve the good experience and how to get rid of bad experience/situation. These understandings prejudice us to consider the experience to be good or bad making us a conditioned person.

For Example, when we look at cow some people may consider it as god, some people may perceive as an object to be eaten. So object is same but the perception is different because of our conditioning.
Developing the sixth sense helps us free ourselves from the clutches of five senses as well as the related prejudice which is created because of the previous experiences.

In our workshops, most of the people can experience the sixth sense in the very first session. One can practice and become the master of sixth sense which can help a person to take right decisions at the right time.
