Techniques:

These below mentioned techniques teach us how to keep our body and mind healthy. These exercises tone up entire body and improve the quality of life and well-being. It develops concentration, confidence, efficiency and more.

These include easy techniques which can be performed in the office, while traveling and even on the bed. Through this one can get rid of toxins and tone-up muscles. This program is very good for arthritis, combating worries and feeling of insecurity, giving meaning and purpose to life, fighting depression and loneliness.

Special yoga techniques:
A series of advanced techniques of yoga are there to work at Annamaya, Pranamaya, Manomaya, Vijnanamaya and Anandamaya Kosas – the five layers of consciousness of human beings called Panca Kosas. It is a part of the IAYT (Integrated Approach of Yoga Therapy) to combat the biggest of killers like Cancer, AIDS.

  1. For Annamaya kosa (at body level):
    •  1. IRT – Instant Relaxation Techniques
    •  2. QRT – Quick Relaxation Technique
    •  3. DRT – Deep Relaxation Technique
    •  4. SMET – Stress Management for Excessive Tension

    2. Cyclicity is one of the key facets of nature. We find Cyclicity present in the nature, internally and externally. For example, earth revolves around sun, stars move in large orbits, human sleep cycles, breathing rhythms, circulatory cycles etc. Any ‘Sadhana’ for inner growth should also incorporate Cyclicity for smooth operation.

    Human tendency is to be in stimulation most of the time and he rarely knows the method of relaxation, cyclic meditation is an art which provides us the adequate knowledge of relaxation technique in spite of various kind of stimuli that tend to in our day- to-day.

    A number of stimulations of varying intensity suited to different levels of stress bring out harmonious growth in tune with one’s innate nature.

  2. For Pranamaya kosa (at vital energy level):
    • PET - Pranic energization technique

    3. PET is an advanced technique of Yoga in which we use our “Prana Shakti” to energize our entire body. Each and every organ of our body can be revitalized by this process which helps on strengthening the immunity.

  3. Manomaya kosa (at mental level):
    • MSRT – Mind and sound resonance technique
      • MSRT is one of the advanced Yoga Techniques for achieving the goal of positive health, will-power, concentration, good memory and deep relaxation. MSRT generates resonance throughout the body by repetition of mental sounds. This helps in revitalizing the internal energy in the body.
    • MIRT – Mind Imagery technique
      • The mind may think in words but the body responds to images as though they are really happening now. The mind talks to the body by using any of the senses and through mental images. Using such imageries or directed visualization in a receptive and related state of mind, one can communicate mental images from the conscious mind to the body and to the unconscious mind. Even Mind imagery techniques have been derived from this principle only. These techniques can produce measurable changes in the body.
    • MEMT – Mastering the emotions technique
      • Mind and emotions have a far-reaching impact on our state of health. They are the internal factors that affect the proper flow of energy and control internal harmony. Most exercises like yoga, qigong and tai chi are to train the mind to be in the mindless state. By clearing the mind from thoughts and emotions, the body can reach a state of calmness so that the energies within it can control each other to achieve harmony and self-healing. “The greatest of things are achieved in a light heart.” Emotional health is vital to a creative mind. There are a number of techniques that allow us to release and resolve emotional issues that impede your personal growth.

      This is the technique not only reveals the scientific basis for how emotions are created and distributed throughout the body, but also gives a number of ways that old emotional baggage from the past can be released from the body/mind network. Emotions that have been suppressed do not just disappear into the ethers. They collect in the body. When emotions of fear, anger, lack or pain are held in the body they impede the ability for the affected organs to do their job efficiently. Since the body is a network of interconnected physical expression, any organ system that has been compromised will weaken the entire system, and reduce mental clarity.

    4. For the creative mind any emotional residues from the past need to be released from the body. There are a number of very good techniques for doing this. Yoga is one of the best, for it stretches all the body structures, relaxes the internal organs and releases stress, along with focused concentration on the wellbeing of the body. Circulation is increased, tension released and the entire organism relaxed and balanced.

  4. Vijnanamaya kosa (at intellectual level):
    • VISAK - Vijanasadhana Kausala
      • One of the conclusions from Upanishad says that fear is present in all of us. Fear is the basic feature of the whole creation. We are fearful of many things. Fear of losing name and fame, near and dear, fear of illness, pain and misery. But the most fundamental fear is death is in built in us. The best way to handle fear is that the question has to be shifted from fear of what to what is fear. Fear is imbalance, terrible speed and constriction, which shakes our existence itself.

      VISAK works on this fear. It provides proper analysis and explanation. Right understanding is the right solution. It gives a way to grow from fear to fearlessness. Both the practice works in two basic area of human life.

    5. ANAMS (explained below) works on happiness whereas VISAK on fear. First of all both provides proper understanding to the subject and clears all the wrong beliefs and ideas. Also provides practical guidelines and practice to grow.

  5. Anandamaya kosa (at spiritual level):
    • VISAK - Vijanasadhana Kausala
      • ANAMS explains everything about happiness, way to experience happiness and also our misconceptions regarding happiness. Along with theoretical explanations it gives the practical direction where, we can make happiness as our basic nature. To be happy we need nothing. All we need is proper understanding and positive practice where mind can be expanded and turned to inner silence.

      The objective of this advanced technique is to make an individual realize that bliss is all pervading, envelopes and indwells everything. The creations is through the bliss. Everything that was, that is, and that will be existing and happening is through bliss. The destruction is also through bliss. The knowledge, the freedom one gets is through bliss.
