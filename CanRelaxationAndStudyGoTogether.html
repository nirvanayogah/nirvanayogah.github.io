Can Relaxation and study go together?:

There are many world famous people who have excelled in different fields but they didn’t have higher education or even failed in few examinations.
For example, Albert Einstein, Thomas Alva Edison, Sir Winston Churchill, Sachin Tendulkar etc. Exams are standardized so much that it is not anymore a measure of student’s ability because a student might be good at few and bad in other subjects.

A student’s success in exams is determined based on his/her luck. Let see the following statistics in the below pie chart:



If a student understands the concepts in a subject then retention is easy which in turn his/her answers and overall performance becomes effective.

There are three stages for attending any exam. Every student can succeed if he/she effectively implements the below steps:
  1. Collection of information
  2. Retention of information
  3. Recollection of information

Effectiveness of the above stages can be improved by yogic techniques.

Requirement of examination are self-confidence, will power, memory, concentration, clarity and understanding which can be improved with the help of yogic techniques like:
  1. Trataka
  2. Palming
  3. Visualization
  4. Vikendrikaran
  5. Asana
  6. Pranayama
  7. Meditation


For guaranteed success, human tendency is to take less demanding ventures. It results in underutilizing our real potential ultimately causing loss of confidence. For such a problem, there is a special yogic technique called “Sankalpa Shakti” which actually goes around experimenting the “sankalpa” (resolution) power in a student.
