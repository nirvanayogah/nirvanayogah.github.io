Yoga for day to day stress related problems:

Every day we have to do various types of work. Our daily hassles includes physical and physiological difficulties like spasms, aching, stiffness, back-pain, general fatigue, feeling “low” which causes mental strain.

For immediate benefit, the time tested “ATM” (Any time two minutes) techniques give instant relaxation.
They are:
  • closing and opening eyes
  • deep breathing
  • mental instructions
  • visualization


For medium and long term benefits, there are various techniques which are useful to reduce down daily stress.
They are:
  • Sukshma vyayama
  • Kriyas
  • Asanas
  • Prayanamas
  • Chanting
  • Meditation
