Pratibha Vinayak Joshi

 
Mrs. Pratibha Vinayak Joshi has been working as yoga instructor at IIIT-Hyderabad for the last 4 years. She has been teaching Yoga for more than 6 years.

She has completed her yoga instructor course from S-VYASA (Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana), Deemed University, Bangalore. She has undergone training on “Naturopathy and Yoga” from Yoga Sadhana Asharam, Jaipur for more than 2 years.

She is a Ph.D scholar in Economics from Nagpur University, Maharashtra. She has pursued M.A. (Economics), from Pune University.

She has published paper in international conference (as a co-author). Publication is “Yoga in pre-school and Primary School curriculum” which was published in the proceedings of 1st international conference on ‘Yoga in Education’ [ICYE-2012], November 27-28, 2012, Tumkur University, Tumkur.

She has an experience of an Administrative Officer for 7 years at Vidya Vikas Children Hospital and Research centre, Nasik. She was also looking after vaccination, OPD, IPD & Administration work.

Along with managing the role of Yoga Instructor, She also worked as a co-ordinator for yukthahar kendra, IIIT - Hyderabad.

She supervised the below activities:


  1. Employee management
  2. Accounts
  3. Inventory management
  4. Purchase and Sales
  5. Supervising other activities like cooking and cleanliness


In Aarogyaa (health center), IIIT-Hyderabad, Mrs Pratibha was the in-charge of ladies section of Naturopathy. She was looking after medicinal plant garden.
