Our Mission:

Our mission is to do something for Mother Nature, Humanity, Nation, Youth/Student community, Employment Generation, Professionals and Elder people.

Nature:
To begin with as we see that few multinational companies are taking hold of everything causing the entire population of the world to depend upon them. This may eventually influence the Nation and its government policies.

For example let’s see how it is done?
Genetically Modified (GM) Seeds are supplied by these Companies which promise the farmer of high productivity. These seeds are modified such that after harvesting these seeds you can’t obtain new set of seeds for the next season. This results in invariable long dependency on such companies for farming. In a nation like India where we had abundance of seeds now we are in such situation that only a meagre percentage of the farmers produce and preserve their own seeds. They charge unreasonable prices for the seeds which were once free.

One of our faculty has submitted a thesis on “Zero Budget Natural Farming” (ZBNF) so that our farmers can be saved from the moneylenders, their debt trap and suicides. For this we have to protect the desi cows (local cows) because without local cows, ZBNF cannot be done. There are many more advantages of cow like its milk, urine, cow dung, etc. are useful for food consumption, medicine, fertilizers and so on. In order to protect cows we want to generate a revenue not by donation but by giving something valuable to the beneficiaries out of which good percentage given to goshalas.
 
 
Humanity:
How lucky we are that we belong to such country where the people believe in the concept of “Vasudaiva Kutumbakam” (means the entire universe is like a family). Here no matter which village you go if a child disturbs a plant in the evening then elders of the house say “Don’t disturb the plant!! They are sleeping”. See the sensitivity of the people towards all living beings. Indians worship ant, plant (tulsi), tree (peepal), birds, animals, moon, sun and we have established a relationship with them we call moon as a maternal uncle.

They get up we say:
“Samudra vasane devi parvatah stana mandale,
vishnu patni namstubyam pada sparsha samakshwame”
We respect our mother earth, mountains and rivers by singing their glory
“Gange chaiva yamune chaiva godavari saraswathi,
narmade sindu kaveri jalasmin kuru yadapi”

Such a well cultured society has been polluted and turned into a frustrated, narrow minded, selfish and unethical society where our daughters are not safe. Parents, Teachers are responsible to mould the future generation into better citizens but they are not teaching the moral values in the name of freedom. Irony is that Parents and Teachers themselves are not following ethical values in their lives.

Our workshops are of very creative and full of various techniques with very good teachers who can do this job well!!
 
 
Youth/Student Community:
Now-a-days, we see the students are carrying the burden of books on their fragile shoulders. Now the field of knowledge has increased so much that it has become a place for expert. We want expert to attend us for no matter what service we need.

Competition among students has tremendous amount of stress which never existed before. To be successful in the life, Students need good physical, mental capacity, better concentration and memory. Here our traditional ancient yogic techniques are of great use. So we have number of workshops for improvement in these areas.
 
 
Professionals:
Now a days, there is a cut throat competition in almost every profession. No matter whether you are doing philanthropic work or at a higher position, the hassles makes a person stressful and leads to various ailments. It is said that the root cause of many diseases is present in the “MIND”. Yoga not only deals with the symptom of the disease but also works on its root cause which is there in the mind by calming it down. We have hundreds of yogic techniques, indoor and outdoor games which helps a person to get rid of stress and diseases to a great extent.
 
 
Old people:
Inspite of having love for the parents they are not able to take care of them physically it is not that we want to propagate vriddhasrama ( old age house ) but inspite of contributing to a great extent for the benefit of child, society and the country now they are homeless and nobody is there to attend them.

In the past we had the concept of joint family but now everyone is living in a nuclear family. Consequently Elderly people are not taken care. In spite of contributing to a great extent for the benefit of child, society and the country now they are homeless and nobody is there to attend them. Our institution wants to be the catalytic agent by transforming a fixed amount of our revenue to some old age homes who are taking care of helpless old people.
 
 
Country:
A Country is not a land with some people, resources, wealth and weapons. A country also has its spirit, a History, a tradition which becomes the recognition of that country. India is one among such highly blessed country of the world. In spite of having so many infiltrations and invasions, the beauty of the country i.e. democracy and spirituality still stand erect.

Mr. Allama Iqbal said:
“Yunan-o-Misr-o-Roma sab mit gaye jahan se ab tak magar,
Hai baki naam-o-nishan hamara,
kuchh baat hai ke hasti mit’ti nahin hamari,
Sadiyon raha hai dushman daur-e-zaman hamara”
This is the great heritage of the country which teaches the entire world about humanity, harmony and spirituality. It is the fundamental kartavya (duty) of every responsible citizen of this country to do something for the cause.
